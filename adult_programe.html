Adult Lap Swimming

Get Fit & Stay Fit With Daily Lap Swimming

Adult lap swimming provides a full-body workout that targets the core, arms, back, and legs. It’s also perfect for strength, aerobic, and low-impact training.

Looking to improve your routine? Visit a Splashes location to learn more about adult lap swimming. Flexible times are available throughout the week to fit your schedule.

Lap Swimming Guidelines

  • Each lane accommodates up to two lap swimmers.
  • Children under 14 must be with a parent and able to swim 200 yards unassisted.
  • Lanes may be limited during shared pool usage.
  • No private swim instruction or coaching is permitted.
  • No lifeguard on duty.

Note: To reserve an entire lane for yourself, book both A and B slots for the time you want.
