Adult Lap Swimming

Get Fit & Stay Fit With Daily Lap Swimming

Adult lap swimming provides a full-body workout that targets the core, arms, back, and legs. It also offers access to various training methods, including strength training, aerobic training, and low-impact training.

If you're looking to improve your workout routine, visit a Splashes location to learn more about adult lap swimming. Swimmers will find flexible times throughout the week to fit their schedules.

Lap Swimming Guidelines

  • Each lane accommodates up to two lap swimmers
  • Children under 14 must be accompanied by a parent and able to swim 200 yards unassisted
  • Lanes are limited during shared pool usage
  • No private swim instruction or coaching is permitted
  • No lifeguard on duty

NOTE: To reserve an entire lane for yourself and avoid sharing, book both A and B slots for your selected time.
