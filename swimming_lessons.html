Which Lesson is Best?

Our Lesson Types

Private Swim Lessons

Our private swim lessons provide one-on-one instruction for students ages three months through adult. Private swim lessons are the best option for accelerated learning. Each lesson will allow our instructors to build on the momentum and skills taught in the previous lesson with minimal downtime. From the basics of putting your face in the water to refining your stroke technique, private lessons will offer the highest rate of progress.

Learn more here

Semi-Private Swim Lessons

At Splashes, our semi-private swim lessons offer students ages 2.5 to 13 the opportunity to interact with another student of a similar age and ability while still receiving personal attention.

Whether you would like to set up a pair child with another student of similar age and ability or would like to take lessons with a friend or sibling, our semi-private lessons are a cost-effective option. Students also have the opportunity to observe one another in the lesson.

Learn more here

Parent & Me Lessons

Our Parent and Me experience lessons follow known as “Mommy and Me” or “Daddy and Me” classes give parents the opportunity to be hands-on with their little one while our instructors facilitate the learning process between you and your child. These classes provide a fun and relaxed environment for you and your child to master age-appropriate swimming milestones.

In our Parent and Me swim lessons, we encourage children ages 3 months through 3 years to participate in water activities alongside a parent. In some programs, we encourage children ages 2.5 and older to participate in parent or private swim lessons.

Learn more here

Stroke Clinic

Splashes’s Stroke Clinic gives students the opportunity to advance their swimming technique while in a group setting. Our Stroke Clinic classes place emphasis on proper technique over speed and are available for students ages 7 and up.

This stroke clinic is strongly encouraged for children who have either been participating in swim lessons or have previously done stroke foundation level swim courses that student progress must reach. From advanced stroke mechanics to kick work and endurance swim exercises, our stroke clinic serves developing and elite level parent, where individual feedback is a great motivator.

Adult Swim Lessons

It’s never too late to learn how to swim! Whether you are looking to learn the basics or improve your technique, we welcome students at all forms of comfort, and a secure instructor will tailor each lesson to your learning abilities. At Splashes, our adult swim lessons make it fun and easy to learn the fundamentals. All students, ages 16+ are welcome in our adult swim program.

From beginners to advanced, our adult swim lessons are a great option for those looking to overcome their fear of the water or who are looking to become stronger swimmers.
